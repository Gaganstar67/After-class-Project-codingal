School Subject Descriptions

Mathematics
A subject where we learn numbers, patterns, shapes, and problem-solving.
Science
A subject where we learn about nature, experiments, space, animals, and inventions.
Coding
A subject where we learn how to create games, apps, websites, and animations.
Art
A subject where we express our ideas through drawing, colouring, and design.
Cookery
A subject where we learn how to prepare delicious and nutritious meals.
Weaving
A subject where we learn how to create textiles and fabrics.
Pottery
A subject where we learn how to create ceramic objects and sculptures.
Music
A subject where we learn about sounds, rhythms, and how to play instruments.
Carpentry
A subject where we learn how to build and repair wooden structures and items.
